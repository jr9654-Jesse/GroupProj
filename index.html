The Problem

Shopping, both virtually and in-store, takes time and can be mentally overloading. Currently, there is no centralized space that allows shoppers to collect clothing from different stores. Additionally, shoppers are unable to fully visualize outfits before buying which can lead to mismatched outfits and unnecessary purchases. This burden falls on the consumer to return unwanted items, in-store or through postal services, and waiting for reimbursement.

The Solution

Create a centralized virtual wardrobe app that allows users to have access and collect items from any online store.

Features:

  • Users have a virtual environment they can access to mix and match items they have selected as well as having AI recommendations to assist them completing outfits.
  • Shoppers will have an organized virtual hub that saves time, money, and mental fatigue all while making fashion more accessible.
  • Users would be able to track prices for price drops, sales, and restocks.

Project Focus

Topic

Wishlist Wardrobe: A virtual wardrobe to make personal styling, online shopping, and reselling more efficient, sustainable, and worth your money.

Problem Statement

Personal styling and online shopping are currently independent experiences and require a lot of time and mental energy. While the rise of artificial intelligence (AI) and augmented reality (AR) have contributed to tools that allow online shoppers to visualize the outfits virtually prior to purchasing [1], there is no commercially available platform that allows shoppers to:

  • Manage their existing wardrobe and styles, virtually
  • Collect and experiment with different outfit combinations from different brands online
  • Sell old garments and pieces to other buyers
  • See how they look on themselves through AR
  • Compare sizes, designs, quality and cost between brands
  • Place their order for the selected items.

Solution

Wishlist Wardrobe is a consolidated platform that combines personal styling and planning with efficient online shopping. Supported by emerging AI and AR tools, the goal is to provide one platform where customers can select from a wide range of clothing brands, mix-and-match pieces, visualize the outfit on themselves, save items for future purchases, and complete their online order.

Building on previous work in this area [2], Wishlist Wardrobe will use AI support to identify differences in sizing patterns between brands, recommend outfits based on the consumer’s need (professional, casual, formal, special event, etc.), and learn from previous trends to differentiate a potentially successful purchase from one that is likely to be returned.

Additionally, there exists a need for users to social and environmental need for users to resell their clothing items [3]. This can be alleviated by giving users the ability to resell their clothing online alongside new items allowing users to match similar styles for a lower price and in a environmentally conscious way.

The platform can also use a camera or recent picture to recommend clothing sizes and learn from consumers’ product reviews to suggest if/when to size up or size down.
